Virtual Interview & Survey AI Platform (Condensed Specs)
1. Overview
Secure, AI-powered web platform for video interviews & surveys.

Real-time & async support, accessibility, fairness, compliance, multi-tenant data isolation.

Integrates external AI/storage providers with auto-fallback.

2. User Roles
Role	Key Permissions
Admin	Full management rights.
Interviewer	Schedule, conduct, edit interviews/questions.
Hiring Manager	View-only (assessments, reports).
Coordinator	Scheduling & logistics support only.
Candidate	Interview participation & results access.
Survey Respondent	Survey participation & results access.
AI System	Question generation, response analysis.
Automated Persona	Interaction with candidates, issue escalation.

3. Team Management
Email invitations, dynamic role assignments.

Audited actions, simplified UI for role management.

4. Data Segregation
Strict organization-based partitioning at app/database layers.

Full audit logging of all data access attempts.

5. Functional Modules
Interview Module:

Video (live & async), mandatory subtitles, optional AI-delivery.

AI-generated adaptive questions, clarifications, real-time monitoring.

Auto/manual scoring, custom rubrics, practice mode, accessibility.

Survey Module:

AI-supported branching questions (text/video), secure distribution.

AI-driven analytics (sentiment, keywords, scoring).

Reporting & Analytics:

Dashboards (metrics/trends), exportable reports.

Calibration tools, analytics-driven insights.

Team & Provider Management:

Role-based team management, external provider integrations, security, logging, failover mechanisms.

Scheduling & Calendar Integration:

Auto-scheduling, calendar sync, automated reminders, rescheduling, buffer intervals.

6. Security & Compliance
End-to-end encryption, immutable audit trails, tamper alerts.

GDPR, CCPA compliance, consent management, secure data retention.

7. AI & Storage Integration
External/open-source AI integration (BYOM supported).

External storage (Mega, Google Drive, etc.) with fallback.
